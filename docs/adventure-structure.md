# Adventure Structure

## Module Title

**Three Keys of Kara-Tur**

## Intended Foundry Package

A Foundry VTT module containing one complete Adventure document plus supporting compendium packs.

## Top-Level Adventure Contents

```text
00 - Start Here
01 - Campaign Overview
02 - Solo Play Rules
03 - Companion NPCs
04 - Act I: The Temple Lie
05 - Act II: Pilgrimage of the Three Keys
06 - Act III: The Breaking Pilgrimage
07 - Act IV: The Fox Behind the Human Face
08 - Factions and Cults
09 - Disaster Avatars
10 - Magic Items and Sealing Arts
11 - GM Secrets
12 - Endings
```

## Journal Page Template

Each major encounter or scene should use this structure:

```text
Read-Aloud
GM Summary
Objectives
Key NPCs
Checks
Combat Setup
Treasure
Consequences
Foundry Links
```

## Compendium Packs

```text
packs/adventures     Adventure document
packs/actors         NPCs, monsters, companions, villains
packs/items          Magic items, sealing arts, boons, hazards
packs/journals       Adventure text and GM references
packs/scenes         Maps and encounter scenes
packs/roll-tables    Random events, omens, complications
packs/playlists      Music and ambience references
packs/macros         Helper automation
```
