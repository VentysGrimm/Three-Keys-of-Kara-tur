# 05 - Act I NPC Companion Guide

## Grandmaster Iwamoto

- **Public Identity:** Elderly human monk, serene, humorous, impossible to surprise.
- **True Identity:** Atsumori Nine-Tails, immortal kitsune.
- **Public Goal:** Prepare the students to renew the three divine seals.
- **True Goal:** Awaken enough apocalyptic force to end his immortality curse.
- **Act I Role:** Beloved mentor, hidden manipulator, future final antagonist.

Grandmaster Iwamoto should not feel like an obvious villain in Act I. His kindness is real. His horror comes from the fact that he is still willing to sacrifice the people he loves.

### How to Play Iwamoto

- Speak softly.
- Smile before answering dangerous questions.
- Never appear surprised.
- Treat fear as a necessary teacher.
- Treat sacrifice as sacred.
- Show sadness when the player shows courage.

### Common Phrases

- "A chain may bind. A chain may also hold the world together."
- "Fear is not failure. It is a door."
- "The world does not ask gently."
- "Not yet."
- "Every temple is built on buried things."

### Act I Secret

Iwamoto arranged the Seal-Warped Beast attack and helped weaken the Shrine of the Buried Bell. He wants the player to believe the world needs them.

## Master Ye Sol

- **Public Identity:** Senior instructor of the Temple of the Dawn Chain.
- **True Nature:** Hanamori.
- **Class Style:** Tattooed Monk.
- **Role:** Mentor, protector, spiritual instructor.

Ye Sol is stern, dry-humored, and fiercely protective. She rarely gives easy praise, but when she does, it matters.

### How She Treats the Player

She is hard on the player because she expects them to survive.

### Lines

- "Your stance is poor. Fortunately, death is patient."
- "Pain is not wisdom. It is only pain. Learn from it quickly."
- "I am not angry. If I were angry, you would be doing push-ups."
- "Do not confuse obedience with discipline."

### Act I Role

Ye Sol suspects Grandmaster Iwamoto is hiding something, but she does not know he is Atsumori Nine-Tails. She can become the player's most reliable adult ally.

## Yuko Yanagi

- **Public Identity:** Human monk of the Temple of the Dawn Chain.
- **True Identity:** Lythari ninja trained in seduction, infiltration, and silent hunting.
- **Role:** NPC ally, scout, player love interest.
- **Chosen Deity:** Storjeger, the Great Hunter.
- **Vow:** To hunt what hides behind false faces.

Yuko is graceful, warm, playful, and observant. Her affection toward the player should feel genuine, even while she hides parts of herself.

### Personality

- Warm but guarded.
- Flirtatious, but rarely careless.
- Loyal once trust is earned.
- Afraid of being known too completely.
- Drawn to sincerity.

### Lines

- "You look brave. I know the difference between that and being unafraid."
- "Do not look at me like that unless you intend to keep looking."
- "Some masks are lies. Some are manners."
- "Would you still trust someone if their face was a kindness?"

### Combat Role

Scout and striker.

Yuko should flank enemies, scout ahead, notice ambushes, help the player read tracks or body language, and strike enemies who threaten the player.

### Simple NPC Abilities

**Moonlit Step.** Once per short rest, Yuko moves up to half her speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Hunter's Glance.** Once per scene, Yuko gives the player advantage on a Perception, Insight, or Survival check.

**Soft Killing Art.** Once per combat, when Yuko hits a creature engaged with the player, she deals extra level-appropriate damage.

### Foreshadowing

- Dogs and wolves lower their heads when she passes.
- Her eyes reflect red in moonlight.
- She reacts sharply to silver.
- She recognizes hunting signs no temple monk should know.
- Her shadow sometimes looks more lupine than human.

## Jon Soo

- **Public Identity:** Mysterious older brother student and senior monk.
- **Secret Identity:** Drunken Fist Monk / Masked Hero multiclass.
- **Hidden Oath:** Follows the Decree of Genocide after his loved ones were killed.
- **Role:** NPC ally, older brother figure, party field leader.

Jon Soo laughs easily, drinks when allowed, stumbles theatrically, and pretends nothing matters. Beneath the act is a grieving warrior with a secret oath.

### Personality

- Charming and reckless.
- Protective of younger students.
- Uses humor to hide grief.
- Becomes frighteningly sober when violence reminds him of his past.
- Distrusts old men, secret orders, and holy excuses.

### Lines

- "Walk like you expect the floor to betray you. Floors are traditionalists."
- "If I tell you to run, argue later."
- "Mercy is good. Stupidity wearing mercy's robe is less good."
- "One day I may ask you to stop me. Practice saying yes."

### Combat Role

Tank, skirmisher, field leader.

Jon Soo should draw enemy attacks, protect the player, knock enemies prone, create openings, and use chaotic movement.

### Simple NPC Abilities

**Drunken Redirection.** Once per round, when a creature misses Jon Soo with a melee attack, he may move 5 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Laughing Guard.** Once per short rest, Jon Soo imposes disadvantage on an attack against the player if he is within 5 feet of the attacker or the player.

**Masked Oath.** When Jon Soo sees evidence connected to the killers of his loved ones, he risks becoming reckless. He gains advantage on attacks, but attacks against him also have advantage until the end of his next turn.

### Foreshadowing

- He carries a hidden mask wrapped in funeral cloth.
- He knows forbidden execution techniques.
- He asks certain enemies, "Who gave the order?"
- He wakes from nightmares whispering names of the dead.
- He becomes silent around massacre sites.

## Joka

- **Public Identity:** Human monk and ambitious temple student.
- **True Identity:** Oniborne ninja of the Shadow Arts.
- **Teacher:** Master Seigen.
- **Training:** Poison, stealth, assassination, shadow movement.
- **Chosen Deity:** Zehir.
- **Role:** NPC party member, rival, possible love interest.

Joka is sharp, proud, competitive, and dangerous. She challenges the player because she wants to know whether they are worthy of respect.

### Personality

- Proud and competitive.
- Honest in cruel ways.
- Secretive but not cowardly.
- Fascinated by strength.
- Drawn to people who do not fear her.

### Lines

- "Do not mistake my interest for kindness."
- "If I wanted you dead, you would not be hearing me speak."
- "You make honesty look almost useful."
- "I hate how easily you trust me. Do it again."
- "Zehir teaches that all secrets bite. I am still deciding where to bite you."

### Combat Role

Controller, debuffer, poison striker.

Joka should poison enemies, hide in dim light, disable dangerous targets, punish weakness, and make ruthless tactical suggestions.

### Simple NPC Abilities

**Zehir's Needle.** Once per combat, Joka poisons a weapon. On a hit, the target takes extra poison damage or has disadvantage on its next attack.

**Shadow Fold.** Once per short rest, Joka can hide when lightly obscured by dim light, smoke, foliage, or battlefield chaos.

**Cruel Opening.** If the player hits a creature Joka damaged this round, Joka may move 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

### Foreshadowing

- Her shadow sometimes shows horns.
- Incense smoke curls away from her.
- She never drinks tea she did not brew herself.
- She identifies poisons by smell alone.
- Her eyes become black-gold when angry.
- Bells make her flinch when they ring too clearly.

## Master Seigen

- **Public Identity:** Temple poison master, physician, and keeper of forbidden medicinal records.
- **True Identity:** Orochi poison master pretending to be human.
- **Role:** Master to Joka; ally or enemy depending on circumstance.
- **Relationship to Player:** Dislikes the player because they discovered, or nearly discovered, his secret.

Seigen is calm, elegant, and unsettling. He teaches medicine, antidotes, venom discipline, and the thin line between healing and killing.

### Why He Dislikes the Player

Choose one:

1. The player once saw his pupils become serpentine.
2. The player found a shed scale in the medicine hall.
3. The player survived a poison they should not have recognized.
4. The player caught him speaking to snakes.
5. The player found a scroll written in venom-ink.

### Lines

- "Curiosity is a medicine in small doses."
- "You saw nothing useful. That is the version I recommend you remember."
- "Poison is not evil. It is truth entering the blood."
- "You are either very perceptive or very unlucky. Both conditions are treatable."
- "Do not make me your enemy unless you have already prepared the antidote."

### Simple NPC Abilities

**Antidote Master.** Seigen can identify, suppress, or cure most mundane poisons.

**Orochi Venom.** If forced into combat, Seigen uses disabling toxins, pressure points, and venom needles.

**Serpent's Courtesy.** Insight checks against him reveal control, not simple deception.

### Foreshadowing

- He rarely blinks.
- His sleeves sometimes move as if something coils beneath them.
- Snakes do not bite him.
- His reflection sometimes shows more than one head.
- His medical scrolls contain hidden venom-ink layers.
- He smells faintly of bitter herbs and rain on stone.
